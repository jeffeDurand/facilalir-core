# Local training data (gitignored)

Place **YOLO-format** datasets here (images + labels), and point your `data.yaml` `path:` at this directory (see `training/bubble_dataset.example.yaml`).

Obtain data from public research datasets or your own labeling — **check each source’s license** before use (e.g. manga/comic datasets may restrict commercial use).

Do not commit large image sets to git; keep them on disk or use your own storage.
